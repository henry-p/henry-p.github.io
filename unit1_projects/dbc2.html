Hamburg, Germany

On this site, I want to give you some information about my hometown Hamburg.
I've been to a lot of places all over the world, but in my opinion, Hamburg is one of the most beautiful cities on the planet. Its first settlements are proven for about 4000 BC. The city is named after the "Hammaburg" which has been built around 800 AD.
Below you can see a photo composition, that shows a few of the many nice/remarkable places in the city.
Some highlights are:
  • The Binnenalster or Inner Alster Lake
  • The Speicherstadt
  • The Port (Top 20 worldwide)
  • The Kiez (Redlight District)
  • The River Elbe
